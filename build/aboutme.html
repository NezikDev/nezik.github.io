About Me

Hi, Im Nezík, Also called Nezzy or Nez! Im a European digital artist who likes to draw fluffy animals and cool characters! I first tried digital art in late 2021, around the time when I found animation memes & MAPs - these little videos were my main inspiration. I didnt draw much and eventually became too shy to share anything anyway, which sadly just demotivated me further. Around early 2023 I discovered Rainworld and their little creatures called slugcats. Those were simple to draw and as I overcame my fear, i kept improving in my skills! Currently I post my art on Bluesky and I also post animations on Youtube.
